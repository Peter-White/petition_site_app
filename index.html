Massachusetts Pet Fox Legalization Petition

Domesticated foxes are becoming some of North America's more popular exotic pets. They're playful, they're friendly, they work well with kids and other pets (except maybe hamsters) and they're absolutely adorable. Think of them like a combination between a cat and a dog, independent but loyal. Most states within the midwest and south have fully legalized the ownership of domesticated foxes, however owning a domesticated fox in most northern states is still illegal. The purposed of this petition is to get ball rolling on legalizing foxes as pets within the northeastern part of this country starting with it's most influential state Massachusetts.
